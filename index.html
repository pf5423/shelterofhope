About

Every young person deserves access to basic necessities

Our Work

We've partnered with the FLITE center in Ft. Lauderdale to raise and gather supplies meant to support underprivileged teens formerly part of the foster care system who have nowhere else to go

Goal

We believe that everyone should be able to have a place in society where they can function, and we are working to ensure that these teenagers are able to have access to the resources and supplies they need

Steps

Working with various high schools in Broward County, Shelter of Hope has organized inter-school drives that aim to raise goods in tandem with the FLITE center, and we always welcome more help
